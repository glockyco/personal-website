Research Focus

My research explores how data mining and data engineering approaches can improve software quality and developer productivity. I combine empirical research methods with backend engineering to build tools and pipelines that extract actionable insight from large-scale software repositories.

Selected Publications
On the Use of Commit-Relevant Mutants ★ Best Paper
JSS 2024
Johann Glock, Coauthor A, Coauthor B

We investigate how commit-relevant mutants can reduce mutation testing costs while maintaining fault-detection effectiveness, finding that focusing on changed code is both practical and sufficient.
